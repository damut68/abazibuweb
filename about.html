About Us

Abazibu, ha!...their origins are mysterious. But history has it that they originated from St. Joseph's Vocational School, Mbarara around the year of Our Lord 2002. They rose from oblivion to become the talk of the school. By the end of their reign, they had left an indelible mark. The UCE results of their candidate year (2003) are the best ever in the history of the school...
